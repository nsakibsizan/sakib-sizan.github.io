Who I Am

I'm Najmus Sakib Sizan, a Software Support Engineer at BJIT Limited with a deep passion for research in Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, IoT, and Blockchain technologies.

I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from Daffodil International University with a CGPA of 3.63/4.00. During my academic journey, I published 8 research papers in reputed journals and conferences, accumulating 92+ citations and achieving an h-index of 5.

My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence and real-world applications — from healthcare AI and sentiment analysis to smart agriculture and decentralized systems.
