// my story

Passionate technologist at the frontier

I'm Najmus Sakib Sizan, a Software Support Engineer at BJIT Limited, currently pursuing an M.Phil in Computer Science and Engineering at Jagannath University, Dhaka, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Md. Abu Layek.

My professional journey spans software support, data migration, and technical implementation across enterprise environments including SAP and Teamcenter ecosystems. I specialise in bridging the gap between complex technical systems and real-world operational needs.

On the research front, my work explores the convergence of IoT, Blockchain, and Machine Learning — particularly how these technologies can secure and automate agricultural and industrial systems. My publications span IEEE, Elsevier, and Springer, accumulating 92+ citations.

"I'm driven by continuous learning and dedicated to applying cutting-edge technology to solve real-world problems. My goal is to make meaningful contributions in the rapidly evolving field of technology and innovation."

Beyond technical work, I've led student communities as President of the Green University Computer Club (GUCC), mentored CSE students, and served as Executive Member of BASIS Student Forum — building a career defined by both technical depth and community impact.
