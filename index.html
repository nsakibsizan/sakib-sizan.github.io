// 01 — about me

Researcher. Engineer. Leader.

A passionate technologist building at the intersection of research and real-world impact.

I'm Najmus Sakib Sizan, a Software Support Engineer at BJIT Limited with a deep passion for cutting-edge research. My work bridges the gap between complex software systems and innovative solutions.

My research focuses on Blockchain, Machine Learning, and IoT — exploring how these technologies can secure and automate systems in Industry 5.0. I've published in IEEE, Elsevier, and Springer venues with 92+ citations.

Previously, I led as President of the Green University Computer Club, mentored students, and coordinated tech operations for BuffaloGrid Bangladesh. I believe technology's greatest power is in solving problems that truly matter.
