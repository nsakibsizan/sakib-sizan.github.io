Who I Am

I'm a Senior Software Support Engineer at BJIT Limited, with a strong background in Product Data Management (PDM) and technical support. I also have a solid research background in Machine Learning, Blockchain, and Post-Quantum Cryptography. I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Jagannath University and am currently pursuing my MPhil in Computer Science at the same university. I'm passionate about bridging the gap between academic research and real-world engineering.

With 98+ citations and 8 publications, my research work spans across AI, Machine Learning, Blockchain, and Post Quantum Cryptography in Smart Agriculture sector.
